### Phase Details

## Phase 1: Groundwork & Architecture
**Goal:** Initialize the React SEO skeleton, branding, and scroll animations
**Requirements:** ARCH-01, ARCH-02, ARCH-03, UI-01, UI-02
**Success Criteria:**
1. Google PageSpeed (or similar tool) confirms HTML contains text content and metadata before JS executes.
2. Web Vitals are GREEN despite the video frames loading.
3. The scroll-driven landing page animation triggers flawlessly on both desktop and mobile.

## Phase 2: Features & CTA
**Goal:** Build out all pages, navigation, and functional Lead Capture/WhatsApp CTA
**Requirements:** UI-03, LEAD-01, LEAD-02, LEAD-03
**Success Criteria:**
1. Navigation successfully routes between Home, About, Services, Planning, Construction, Contact, and Handover without 404s.
2. Clicking the WhatsApp icon or "Get a Quote" opens the respective prompt or mailto correctly on all devices.

## Phase 3: Experience & Optimization
**Goal:** Integrate background audio, enforce global theme guidelines, and optimize performance for PageSpeed/SEO.
**Requirements:** UI-04, UI-05, ARCH-04
**Success Criteria:**
1. A background audio track automatically plays (or invites play) with a visible mute/unmute toggle control.
2. New pages and components map strictly to the existing amber/black premium theme without inline style deviations.
3. PageSpeed Insights scores are demonstrably green (>90) for the newly introduced components.
